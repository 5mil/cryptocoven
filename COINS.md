# Mining-Dutch Coin Reference

Full list of coins available on [Mining-Dutch.nl](https://www.mining-dutch.nl), with algorithm, current USD price, exchange availability, difficulty tier, block probability, and Lazy Mining (auto-swap) eligibility.

> Prices sourced from Mining-Dutch live prices page. "none" in the Exchange column means no active market listing was found on the pool.
> **Lazy Mining** = Mining-Dutch's auto-swap feature. Any coin with an active exchange listing is eligible. Coins with `none` exchange cannot be auto-swapped.
> **Block Probability** is rated relative to network hashrate vs pool hashrate: 🟢 High (low difficulty, small network), 🟡 Medium, 🔴 Low (massive network like BTC/ETH).
